OUR PHILOSOPHY

  • COMMAND & CONTROL

    When employees have the self-conception of classic implementers who thrive in a hierarchical top-down model, we talk about the "command and control" system.

  • SHADOW ORGANIZATION

    Likewise, the shadow organization is a system creating major organizational problems due to a mismatch between organizational design and self-conception of employees.

  • OVERSTRAINED ORGANIZATION

    A lack of congruence between organizational design and employee self-understanding gives rise to problem areas, systems with suboptimal performance, low output, and a lack of employee motivation.

MANAGEMENT TOOLS

This is the reality in German companies. However, the majority of companies can currently only partially meet the highly dynamic requirements of the market because they can only exercise command and control using the existing management tools. Agile, fast, market-oriented approaches are either not or only partially possible in this system.

STATUS QUO

But which of these systems is right for my business? There is no right answer, since all these systems happen to coexist in almost every company. It is this very coexistence, this interplay of many different forms of work organization that makes companies more productive and efficient.

AGILE NETWORK

Agile networks are produced when flexible organizational design and self-responsible highly ambitious employees merge. Agile networks are characterized by a management’s deep trust in the abilities of the team and employees to recognize the needs of the market and autonomously implement them.
